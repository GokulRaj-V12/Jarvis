The Problem

😩

Lost Progress

People lose track of daily achievements, goals, and patterns. Traditional journals are passive.

🤖

No Context

Generic AI doesn't know your goals, mood history, or past wins. Every chat starts from zero.

⏰

Not Proactive

AI tools wait for you to open them. A real companion should reach out with plans automatically.

The Solution

🧠

Persistent Memory

Jarvis remembers your logs, goals, and blockers across every single conversation.

🎯

Personalized AI

Uses RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) to pull relevant memories into every response.

⏰

Proactive

Automatically sends a morning game plan at 8 AM and an evening review at 9 PM.
